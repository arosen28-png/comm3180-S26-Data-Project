## Data analysis notebooks for COMM3180 Group Data Project

This folder contains the Jupyter notebooks used to clean, analyze, and visualize data related to our project on housing, population, and economic activity in Philadelphia, with a focus on University City.

* The goal of this analysis is to understand how population patterns, housing costs, and business activity shape living conditions in Philadelphia.

---

### Beginning_Outline
- Notebook outlining our project idea, research questions, and planned approach  
- Includes initial discussion of datasets and how they connect to our research focus  

### Phillyanalysis
- Main notebook used to explore and clean all Philadelphia datasets  
- Includes:
  - Household structure data (family vs non-family households)
  - Population data (total population and gender distribution)
  - Rent data (housing cost distribution)
  - University City dataset (comparison between UCity and Philadelphia)
  - Business dataset (economic activity by zip code)

- Key steps:
  - Data cleaning (renaming columns, handling missing values)
  - Creating new variables (percentages, differences, groupings)
  - Descriptive analysis and visualizations

- Key outputs:
  - Rent distribution across Philadelphia
  - Comparison of University City vs Philadelphia across key metrics
  - Business activity by zip code


### Overall Project Focus
Our analysis examines how housing affordability, population structure, and economic activity interact to shape neighborhood dynamics. University City serves as a case study to highlight how specific areas can differ from broader city trends.
